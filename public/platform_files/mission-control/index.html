MISSION CONTROL

 
 
 

VISIBILITY, TRANSPARENCY, and CONTROL!  Ylopo has built the industry's first sophisticated digital marketing dashboard that's designed to be run in either Co-Pilot or Auto-Pilot modes.  Co-Pilot is for our Ylopo clients who want to get their hands dirty and easily create and manage their own digital marketing campaigns and Auto-Pilot for those clients who want to leave all the digital marketing management and creation up to the experts here at Ylopo.

Request Demo
 
 

THE POWER OF A SOPHISTICATED MARKETING PLATFORM PACKAGED IN EASY TO DIGEST USER INTERFACE

With Mission Control you'll never have to worry about figuring out Facebook Ad Manager or Google Adwords or trying to keep up with the latest trends in digital marketing.  Our platform reduces the most complicated marketing decisions into a user-friendly dashboard that gives you full control and visibility into your marketing, while always having the support of our dedicated Ylopo marketing team who's optimizing your campaigns every single day.

 
 

CONTROL WHEN YOUR ADS ARE RUNNING, HOW MUCH YOU SPEND AND WHERE YOUR LEADS ARE TARGETED

The most important marketing decisions for any successful agent involves how much to spend at any given time on marketing, what homes or branded messages to advertise, to which demographic groups you’d like to target and to what geographic locations you’d like to reach.  With Ylopo Mission Control all of these options are just ONE click away, giving you ultimate control in an easy to use dashboard.

 

COMPLETE VISIBILITY INTO WHAT ADS ARE RUNNING AND TO WHAT DEMOGRAPHIC GROUPS ARE BEING TARGETED

With Ylopo, digital marketing is no longer a "black box" where you just have to trust an outside service provider that they are doing the best possible job.  With Mission Control, you have total visibility, transparency, and  access to the ads we're running on your behalf.  You can edit every part of the ad down to the photos and message on each listing being advertised as well as manage what demographic groups the ad is being shown.
