CRM Integration

 
 

Don’t be held prisoner by one CRM. Take advantage of the best in breed technologies that increase your team's efficiency and productivity.  Ylopo has the most in-depth integrations with leading real estate industry CRM's.

Request Demo
 

SEAMLESS TWO WAY INTEGRATION WITH YOUR CRM OF CHOICE!

We carefully vet each of our CRM partners to ensure that they meet our strict usability standards and have the technical capability to create deep integrations.  The result is a seamless experience between your CRM and Ylopo Stars - our consumer intelligence platform (see more below). 

 
 

YLOPO STARS GIVES YOU FULL VISIBILITY AND CONTROL OVER YOUR PROSPECTS' BEHAVIOR


With one click from your CRM, you'll be able to monitor the behavior of your prospect throughout their entire home search experience.  24/7, see exactly what they searched for, favorited, shared with spouses, etc. all organized to maximize your insight into what types of homes they really want to see.  Based on your direct interactions with your buyer, you can easily edit their listing alerts and send on-demand push listings via email and text!

 

AUTOMATICALLY SETUP LISTING ALERTS FOR ANY TYPE OF LEAD IN YOUR CRM!

Rest assured that when leads are sent to your CRM from other lead sources such as Zillow, Trulia, or Realtor.com, Ylopo immediately detects the new lead and creates an on-target automatic listing alert campaign for that lead!  You can relax knowing that our technology is nurturing every lead regardless of whether it's generated from Ylopo or not!
